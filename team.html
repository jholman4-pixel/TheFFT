Identity

We are The Florida Football Team—a statewide identity built by fans. Our look is NFL-style: a modern shield, bold typography, and a powerful color palette.

  • Primary Colors: Navy (#0b2a3b), Orange (#f26522), Blue (#1b4761)
  • Wordmark: “FLORIDA” / “FOOTBALL” stacked within the shield
  • Style: Clean, modern, and highly legible for broadcast and merchandise

Mascot: Florida Man

Our mascot is an epic, heroic take on Florida Man—charging through hurricane winds, flag in hand. It’s a tribute to the state’s resilience and humor.

Community

Grassroots first. Our roadmap includes youth football grants, coastal cleanup days, and open training camps across the state.
